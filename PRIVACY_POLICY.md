# Privacy Policy for Breathe Before Buy

**Last Updated: October 19, 2024**

## Introduction

Breathe Before Buy ("we", "our", or "the extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how our Chrome extension handles your information.

## Information We Collect

### What We Collect
The extension stores the following information **locally on your device only**:
- Breathing exercise preferences (number of breath cycles)
- Inhale duration setting (in seconds)
- Exhale duration setting (in seconds)
- Extension enabled/disabled state
- Session storage of checkout page identifiers (to prevent duplicate triggers)

### What We DON'T Collect
- We do NOT collect any personally identifiable information
- We do NOT track your browsing history
- We do NOT record what websites you visit
- We do NOT collect information about your purchases
- We do NOT track what items you're buying
- We do NOT access your payment information
- We do NOT collect your email, name, or contact details
- We do NOT use cookies or tracking technologies

## How We Use Information

The information stored locally is used solely to:
1. Remember your breathing exercise preferences
2. Customize the breathing duration to your liking
3. Prevent the breathing overlay from appearing multiple times for the same checkout session
4. Enable or disable the extension based on your preference

## Data Storage

- **All data is stored locally** using Chrome's local storage API
- **Nothing is transmitted** to external servers
- **No analytics** are collected or sent anywhere
- **No third-party services** are used
- The extension works completely offline after installation

## Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade any information because we simply don't collect any information that could identify you.

**We have ZERO access to:**
- Your shopping cart contents
- Your purchase history
- Your payment information
- Your personal details
- Your browsing activity

## Permissions Explained

### Storage Permission
- **Why we need it**: To save your breathing exercise settings (breath count, inhale/exhale duration)
- **How we use it**: Settings are stored in Chrome's local storage on your device only
- **What we don't do**: We never sync data to the cloud or external servers

### Active Tab Permission
- **Why we need it**: To detect when you're on a checkout page and display the breathing overlay
- **How we use it**: The extension checks if the current page is a final checkout page
- **What we don't do**: We never track which sites you visit or record your activity

### Host Permissions (<all_urls>)
- **Why we need it**: To work on all e-commerce websites (Amazon, eBay, Etsy, Shopify stores, etc.)
- **How we use it**: The extension needs to detect checkout pages across different shopping sites
- **What we don't do**: We never collect data from websites, only detect checkout page patterns

## Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party services, including:
- No analytics platforms (Google Analytics, etc.)
- No crash reporting services
- No advertising networks
- No social media integrations
- No external APIs or servers

## Children's Privacy

This extension does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The extension is designed for adults making online purchases.

## Data Retention

Since all data is stored locally on your device:
- Data persists until you uninstall the extension or clear Chrome's local storage
- You can reset all settings by uninstalling and reinstalling the extension
- No data is retained by us because we never receive any data

## Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:
- **Access**: View your settings by clicking the extension icon
- **Modify**: Change your breathing preferences at any time
- **Delete**: Uninstall the extension to remove all locally stored data
- **Opt-out**: Disable the extension at any time through the settings

## Security

- All data remains on your device using Chrome's secure local storage
- No data transmission means no risk of data interception
- No servers mean no server-side security vulnerabilities
- The extension code is client-side only
