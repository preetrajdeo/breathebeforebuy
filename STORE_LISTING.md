## Detailed Description (16,000 characters max)

### Stop Impulse Buying with Mindful Breathing

Breathe Before Buy helps you make more intentional purchasing decisions by introducing a simple pause before you complete online transactions. When you're about to place an order, the extension automatically guides you through a calming breathing exercise, giving you a moment to reflect on whether you really need the item.

### How It Works

1. **Automatic Detection** - The extension recognizes when you're on a final checkout page (Amazon, eBay, Etsy, Shopify, and more)

2. **Gentle Pause** - After a few seconds, a beautiful full-screen breathing overlay appears

3. **Guided Breathing** - Follow the animated breathing circle through customizable breath cycles
   - Visual cues for inhaling and exhaling
   - Peaceful meditation character animation
   - Progress tracking to count your breaths

4. **Mindful Reflection** - After breathing, you're asked: "How do you feel about this purchase now?"
   - Continue if you still want to buy
   - Or take more time to think about it

### Key Features

**Automatic Intervention**
- Detects final checkout pages across major e-commerce sites
- Triggers automatically before you can complete the purchase
- No manual activation needed

**Customizable Breathing Exercise**
- Choose 3-10 breath cycles
- Adjust inhale duration (4-8 seconds)
- Adjust exhale duration (4-8 seconds)
- Settings accessible from the breathing overlay

**Beautiful, Calming Design**
- Smooth animations with expanding/contracting breathing circle
- Peaceful meditation character
- Clean, distraction-free overlay
- Progress bar to track your breathing journey

**Smart Detection**
- Works on Amazon, eBay, Etsy, Shopify stores, and more
- Detects "Place Order", "Buy Now", "Complete Purchase" buttons
- Only triggers on final checkout pages (not on cart or shipping pages)
- Prevents duplicate triggers for the same order

**Privacy-Focused**
- All data stored locally on your device
- No tracking or analytics
- No data sent to external servers
- Works completely offline after installation

### Why Breathe Before Buy?

**Combat Impulse Buying**
Research shows that a simple pause can significantly reduce impulse purchases. By taking just a minute to breathe and reflect, you create space between the urge to buy and the actual purchase.

**Save Money**
Users report avoiding unnecessary purchases and feeling more confident about the items they do buy. That "buy now" pressure fades when you take a moment to breathe.

**Reduce Decision Fatigue**
Online shopping can be overwhelming. A breathing break helps clear your mind and make more intentional choices.

**Build Mindfulness**
Regular breathing practice, even in small moments, can help develop overall mindfulness and self-awareness.

### Get Started

1. Install the extension
2. Visit any online checkout page
3. When you're about to place an order, the breathing exercise will automatically appear
4. Follow the breathing animation
5. Reflect on your purchase decision
6. Choose to continue or reconsider

- Adjust the breath count and duration to what feels comfortable

## Notes for Chrome Web Store Review

This extension is designed to promote mindful consumer behavior and financial wellness. It does not:
- Block or interfere with legitimate purchases
- Collect any user data
- Track browsing history
- Inject ads or promotional content

The extension only activates on final checkout pages and provides a voluntary breathing exercise. Users can skip the exercise at any time using the "Skip" button.

All functionality is purely client-side with no external server communication.
